Implications - Big Data

Aspect Advantages Disadvantages
Society Enhances public decision-making by providing accurate and relevant data. Risks to privacy and personal data security.
Economy Allows optimization of resources and operational efficiency in businesses. Can create economic inequalities due to the digital divide.
Science Facilitates the analysis of large data volumes for advanced research, such as in astronomy and genetics. Requires significant investments in infrastructure and technology.
Technology and Innovation Promotes the development of new technologies and methodologies, such as parallel processing. Rapid technological evolution can render investments and systems obsolete.
