Implications - Big Data

Aspect Advantages Disadvantages
Scalability Handles growing volumes of data without losing performance. Requires significant investment in scalable infrastructure and technologies.
Fraud Detection Enhances the ability to detect irregular patterns and fraud in real-time. Complexity and volume of data can lead to false positives, increasing operational costs.
Accessibility Facilitates access to relevant information for users regardless of location. Differences in infrastructure and access capabilities can create a digital divide.
Task Automation Allows automation of complex processes and repetitive tasks, improving operational efficiency. Automation may reduce the need for labor, potentially impacting employment in certain sectors.
Resource Optimization Helps maximize the use of available resources, reducing costs and increasing efficiency. Can lead to overreliance on data, overlooking other important qualitative factors.
Continuous Improvement Enables ongoing analysis and enhancement of products, services, and processes based on data. Constant need for updates can be costly and complex to manage.
